# Open Source Developer of the Year!

<p class='italic'>March 2, 2026</p>

<div class='page-pic'><a href='/assets/img/open-source-developer-of-the-year.jpg'><img src="/assets/img/open-source-developer-of-the-year.jpg" alt="The PHP Elephants are so proud of the Open Source Developer of the Year award!" title="The PHP Elephants are so proud of the Open Source Developer of the Year award!"></a><p>The PHP Elephants are so proud of the Open Source Developer of the Year award!</p></div>

At [the Confoo web technology conference in Montreal](https://confoo.ca){:target="_blank"}, Formulize Lead Developer, Julian Egelstaff, won the Open Source Developer of the Year award!

The beautiful glass award now has a proud place in the office where the magic happens. The PHP Elephants love it.

Formulize developer Arvin Singla was there at the conference and spoke many kind words of introduction. Julian thanked all the contributors to Formulize.

"It's clich&eacute;, but I stand on the shoulders of giants. This is very validating, of all the work that we've done this past year, and for the past 22 years," he said.
